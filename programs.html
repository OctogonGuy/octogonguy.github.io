Java

Programs I've made in Java. You should hopefully be able to open any of these .jar files by just double clicking them. However, if you are using a Windows computer, it may not work for some reason. If that is the case, you may need to open it through the command prompt. If you do not know how to do this, perform the following steps:

  1. Open "Command Prompt"
  2. Type "cd [directory to the .jar file]" and press Enter
  3. Type "java -jar [filename]" and press Enter

Backgammon

A backgammon game. Nothing special really. Click the dice to roll. When trying to move, click the piece you want to move and then click the space you would like to move to. If you click a piece by mistake, you should be able to click on any non-movable space to go back.

Battleship

A basic Battleship game. At the beginning, when you are placing your ships, click the ship you want to place and then click the space on the board you where would like to place it. To rotate the currently selected ship, use the arrow keys or the scroll wheel.

Connect 4
